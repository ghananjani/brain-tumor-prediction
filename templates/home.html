BRAIN TUMOR DETECTION SYSTEM

INTRODUCTION

Brain tumour is a mass or growth of abnormal cells in your brain. Brain tumors can begin in your brain (primary brain tumors), or cancer can begin in other parts of your body and spread to your brain as secondary (metastatic) brain tumors.

Aim is to build an efficient method which helps in the segmentation and detection of the brain tumour without any human assistance based on Convolutional Neural Network.
